Content Strategy

This site will be a single page with the following sections:

Header/Banner:

A custom banner containing the Squidly Designs name brand, logo, and a short description of the site's purpose: custom art commissions.

About Me:

A brief write-up on Jennifer Hernandez containing a small bio with an emphasis on her love of animals. A picture of Jennifer in her art booth and a photo of her home studio will highlight the section. This will help the potential customer feel closer to the artist and put a face to the creator of the personalized portrait.

Portfolio:

Several examples of Jennifer's previously commissioned pet portraits. Each image will have a brief description highlighting the style of the portrait, giving the customer more options and ideas.

Contact:

A contact form for the potential customer to submit an inquiry for a commission. This will include name, contact info, a textarea to describe what they are looking for, and an option to submit a photo of their pet. Each submission is unique, requiring direct communication with Jennifer to make a truly unique portrait and experience.
