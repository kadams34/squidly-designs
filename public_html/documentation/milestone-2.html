Content Strategy

This site will be a page with the following sections:

Header/Banner: A custom banner containing the Squidly Designs name brand and a one line description of the site's purpose: custom art commissions.

About Me: A brief write-up on Jennifer Hernandez containing a small bio and a section on her love of animals. A picture of Jennifer in her art booth will highlight the section. This will help the potential customer feel closer to the artist and put a face to the creator of the personalized portrait.

Portfolio: Several examples of Jennifer's pet portraits. Each image will have a description highlighting the style of the portrait, giving the customer more options.

Contact: A contact form for the potential customer to submit an inquiry for a commission. Each submission is unique, requiring direct communication from Jennifer to the client.
