Purpose, Audience, Goals, and Persona

Purpose:

This PWP will be a portal for customers to submit commission requests from local Albuquerque artist Jennifer Hernandez, owner of Squidly Designs.

Audience:

The site will be used by the general public and potential customers. Designed for current and future fans and customers wanting a personalized drawing or painting. Target audience will have an interest in supporting local art communities and handmade, original products.

Goals:

The primary goal of the site will be to increase sales of commissions and to provide customers with a contact portal to formally make commission requests. Commission requests are currently done through social media and email. This website will help organize requests, increase productivity, and will enable the business to handle more requests.

Persona:

  • Name: Sadie Spencer
  • Age: 25
  • Occupation: Sadie is a student studying art history, a server at a locally owned breakfast and lunch diner, and a volunteer at a local animal shelter.
  • Technology: mobile- iPhone 11 laptop: 2019 MacBook Pro OS: macOS Catalina
  • Behaviors & Attitudes: Sadie LOVES animals of all kinds. Lives downtown in a loft with her boyfriend. Always shops local and prefers to steer clear of corporate entities and chains. Prefers sustainable, organic(if possible), and handmade products and crafts. Shops at outdoor farmer's markets as often as possible. Has a very busy schedule with work, school, and her volunteer work, but makes a point to support local artists, music, and other events that benefit her community.
  • Frustrations & Needs: May is a very busy celebration month for her family. 2 birthdays in total, not including her own, with Mother's Day as well. With finals at school coming up, a busier schedule at the diner from the warming weather, and no desire to buy mass produced products, finding gifts for her family members' birthdays and Mother's Day will be a challenge.
  • Goals: Sadie's goal for using this site will be to find a gift that suits her family members' personalities. Sadie, as well as her family, loves animals and every family member has a pet or pets of their own. While shopping at the Downtown Albuquerque's Grower's Market, she stumbled upon Squidly Designs' booth and really enjoyed Jennifer's art. After taking Jennifer's card and following Squidly Designs on Instagram, she saw the option for personalized pet portraits. This website will allow her to place an order for a personalized drawing of each of her family members' pets.

User Story: As a new user, Sadie needs to place an inquiry for a personalized art commission.
