A Little Bit About Me

Hello! I’m Jennifer, the artist behind Squidly Designs.

I was born and raised in New Mexico and I currently live in Albuquerque. I started Squidly Designs while I was a Graphic Design undergrad at New Mexico State University in Las Cruces. My dream at the time was to someday operate my own little design firm working as a freelance graphic and web designer.

Instead Squidly Designs has become my own brand of kawaii greeting cards, art prints, and other products. I’ve always loved children’s books and films with their whimsical and humorous characters in their brightly colored worlds. I wanted to create art that makes people squeal with delight at the amount of cuteness or laugh out loud at the humorous scenarios I create with my characters. My ultimate goal is to make people feel happy when they see my art.
